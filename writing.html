Master Urdu script with interactive exercises

🖊️ How to Practice Writing

  1. Trace letters - Follow the guides carefully
  2. Write slowly - Focus on correct form
  3. Practice daily - 10-15 minutes is enough
  4. Get feedback - Use AI Study Buddy to check
  5. Be patient - Script mastery takes time!

Remember: Urdu is written from right to left →

Paragraph Writing

Topic: میرا خاندان (My Family)

Write a short paragraph (4-5 sentences) about your family.

Hints: - میرے والد کا نام… (My father’s name is…) - میری والدہ… (My mother…) - میرے بھائی/بہن… (My brother/sister…) - ہم سب… (We all…)

📝 Writing Tips

🖊️ Perfect Your Writing

  1. Start Right: Begin from the right side of the page
  2. Connect Letters: Most letters connect to the next
  3. Mind the Dots: Dots placement is important (ب ت ث)
  4. Practice Ligatures: Common letter combinations
  5. Use Guidelines: Keep letters aligned
  6. Write Slowly: Speed comes with practice
  7. Review Regularly: Check your old writing

Pro Tip: Write on lined paper turned 90° for better guides!
